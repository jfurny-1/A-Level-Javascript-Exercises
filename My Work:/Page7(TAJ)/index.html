The Taj Mahal

Click Me Location: The Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India. Purpose: It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Construction: Construction began in 1632 and took approximately 22 years to complete. Complex: The Taj Mahal stands in the middle of a complex covering 170,000 m². Visitors: Between 7 to 8 million people visit the Taj Mahal in a single year.
