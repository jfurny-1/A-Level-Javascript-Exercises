The Colloseum

Click Me It was built between 72 A.D. and 80 A.D. under Emperor Vespasian. Made from stone and concrete, it is the largest amphitheater in the world. It could host 50,000 spectators and had 80 entrances. Shows often lasted all day from dawn to dusk. 60,000 slaves (mostly Jewish) built the Colosseum, finishing it in under 9 years
